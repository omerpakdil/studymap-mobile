StudyMap Support

Need a hand with StudyMap Pro? Review the quick fixes below or contact our team for one-on-one assistance.

Quick Start Checklist

Install the Beta

Open the TestFlight invitation email and tap View in TestFlight. Install StudyMap Pro and sign in with your account to sync existing study plans.

Activate Premium

Go to the Premium tab inside the app to start a Weekly, Monthly, or Annual plan once subscriptions become available in TestFlight.

Stay Updated

Enable TestFlight notifications so you receive new beta builds, release notes, and known issue updates as soon as they are published.

Account & Sign-In Help

  • Confirm you are using the same email address you registered with; StudyMap currently supports sign-in with email and magic link verification.
  • Check your spam folder if you do not receive the verification email within two minutes. The email subject line is “Confirm your StudyMap login”.
  • For access resets, contact us at callousity@gmail.com with the subject line “Account access request”.

Subscriptions & Billing

  • Manage your subscription at any time under Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iOS device.
  • Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle; premium access continues until that date.
  • Apple handles billing and refunds for in-app purchases. For refund requests, visit reportaproblem.apple.com.
  • If no plans appear in the app, Apple may still be reviewing the subscription products. We will notify you through TestFlight once they are approved.

Troubleshooting Steps

Connection Errors

Switch to a different Wi-Fi or cellular network, disable VPN, then relaunch the app. Persistent issues may indicate backend maintenance—check TestFlight notes for status.

Missing Data

Verify you are logged into the correct StudyMap account. Pull-to-refresh on the Dashboard to sync with the server.

Send Feedback

From TestFlight, tap Send Beta Feedback and attach screenshots or screen recordings. Include device model and iOS version for faster triage.

Need More Help?

Email our support team at callousity@gmail.com. We typically respond within one business day (Monday–Friday, 9:00–18:00 GMT+3). Please include:

  • Your StudyMap account email address
  • The device model and iOS version you are using
  • A short description of the issue and the steps to reproduce it

For urgent incidents affecting multiple users, add “URGENT” to your email subject so we can prioritize your request.
