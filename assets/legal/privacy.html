StudyMap Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2025

1. Overview

StudyMap is committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains the information we collect, how we use it, and the circumstances under which we may share it when you use the StudyMap mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: First name, last name, email address, and other data you provide during onboarding.
  • Onboarding Inputs: Exam goals, subject intensity, target exam date, preferred study schedule, and learning style preferences.
  • Usage Data: Completed tasks, reminder settings, interaction logs, and generated study programs.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and identifiers provided by Apple.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized study plans and content via the Google Gemini API.
  • To schedule reminders, notifications, and weekly progress summaries.
  • To manage subscriptions through RevenueCat and Apple’s in-app purchase system.
  • To analyze app performance and improve user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent abuse of the service.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We may share personal data with third parties solely to provide the StudyMap service:

  • Google Gemini: Onboarding details (such as subjects and schedules) to generate study content.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription status management, purchase validation, and entitlement tracking.
  • Apple: Notification permissions and in-app purchase processing.

These providers may only use your data to deliver their respective services and are bound by their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your data by contacting callousity@gmail.com.

6. Data Security

StudyMap uses TLS/SSL encryption for data in transit and relies on operating system safeguards for data stored on your device. However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

If you reside in jurisdictions such as the EU/EEA, you may have the right to:

  • Access, update, or correct your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict data processing.
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at callousity@gmail.com.

8. Children’s Privacy

StudyMap does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any significant changes will be communicated via in-app notice or email where appropriate.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please email callousity@gmail.com.
